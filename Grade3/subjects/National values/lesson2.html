Highway codes

Lesson 2

Highway codes are rules and regulations that govern road use.

The highway codes of Nigeria are the rules and regulations that enforce the proper use of the road by motorists and pedestrians to ensure safety. Nigerian rules apply only in Nigeria. Other countries have their own highway codes. For example, British high way codes state that the driver's seat must be on the right side of the car. 

The constituted authority of the Nigerian highway codes is the Federal Road safety Commission (FRSC) and the Vehicle Inspection Office (VIO). The police and army also assist to enforce the laws that govern the use of Nigerian roads. Their main function is to ensure safety and educate more people about the highway codes of Nigeria.

Road safety Officers

Vehicle Inspection Officers

Various kinds of people make use of the roads each day. It is very important to always pay attention to signs on the road and the traffic lights for your safety and for the safety of others.

Did you know?

"... that it is illegal for a person to drive a car on the road without a driver's license"

Before you cross the road, 'LOOK LEFT, LOOK RIGHT, THEN LOOK LEFT AGAIN!' When the road is clear, then you can cross the road. 

Road Signs

Road signs or traffic signs are signs erected at the side or above roads to provide information to road users. The FRSC and VIO use road signs as a non-verbal means of communicating the rules of the road.

It is important to learn the meaning of road signs to that you can easily identify and understand when you see them on the road. 

Rules and regulations of the road
  • 1. All drivers must be above 18 years and must own a drivers license
    2. Drivers and passengers must always wear a seat belt
    • 3. Make sure your vehicle is in good working condition

    4. Always obey the traffic lights 

    5. Do not drive above the speed limit

    6. Do not drink alcohol and drive

    7. Never cross the road without looking left, right and left again

    8. Use the pedestrian bridge or the zebra crossing
