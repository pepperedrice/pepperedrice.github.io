Political Map of West Africa

West Africa is a sub-region of the African continent. There are Sixteen independent countries in west Africa. Many countries in west Africa share borders with the Atlantic ocean. Our country Nigeria is also located in west Africa.Many countries in west Africa are rich in minerals and other natural resources. 

Nigeria's Neighbors

Nigeria's neighbors are countries that share boundaries with her. By observing the political map at the top of this page, the Republic of Niger is to the north of Nigeria, Chad is towards the north-east, Cameroun covers the entire east and south east of Nigeria. The Atlantic ocean from the south to the south-western border of Nigeria where Lagos is located. Benin Republic is to the west of Nigeria. 

Did you know?

"... that all of Nigeria's Neighbors are french speaking countries. "

ECOWAS
Economic Community Of West African States

The aims of the Community are to promote cooperation  of west African countries and promote progress.


West African Countries

There are 16 countries in West Africa with a total population of over 360 million people. French Speaking countries use the west African CFA Franc which is the official currency of the French colonies in west Africa.

The list of West African countries and their currencies shows that eight of  16 countries in West Africa use the West African Franc CFA as their official currency. This was introduced as a common currency to the former french colonies back in 1945. And even after gaining independence, the CFA remained in use.
