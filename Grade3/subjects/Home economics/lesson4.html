Food items in the Locality

Food is one of the basic needs of all living things. Plants and animals are main sources of food. Food items are organic materials we use to prepare food that we can eat.

Food can be eaten in cooked and uncooked forms. Uncooked foods are known as raw foods. The food gets soft and tasty when we cook and can also be easily be digested. 

All foods items get spoilt over a period of time. We know that the food is spoilt if there is a bad smell coming from food and if there is bacteria growing on it. Food will be spoiled if it is not prepared, preserved and handled in the correct way. 

In Nigeria, certain food items are common to different localities. There are two sources of food - plants and animals. All the food we eat comes from either of these two. The processed foods we eat in our daily lives (like chips, cakes, pizzas, sandwiches, etc) is a combination of various ingredients that come from either plants or animals. 

Cooking is the practice or skill of combining, mixing and heating food ingredients in the right quantity to prepare a meal.
