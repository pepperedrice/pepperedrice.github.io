Home Economics

A house

Lesson 1

A house is a building where people live

Its a place where individuals or families can find shelter. A house is regarded as a home when a family stays together in peace and harmony.

People live in different kinds of houses. Many people keep on moving from one place to another. A house could either be a temporary or permanent place to live. The houses we live in protects us from heat, rain, wind, dust and animals. Different kinds of houses are built in different regions of the world depending on the climate of that region.

Did you know?

"... that houses are made of wood, bricks, cement, iron, glass and even snow!"

Our house protects us from the sun, rain, wind, snow, animals and people that try to steal our things. 

Windows allow dirty and dusty air to go out and clean air to come in. They also allow sunlight to enter the house.

We must keep our house and its surroundings clean from dirt and garbage so that we dont get infected by germs and diseases. 

Types of houses

Bungalow

It is a house that usually has one storey. 

Duplex

It is a house that usually has more than one storey. A duplex has a staircase that leads upstairs

Mansion

A mansion is a house that is very big and expensive. It has many rooms and bathrooms

A Hut

This is a traditional house made from local materials like clay, palm trees and sticks.

Igloo

Its a circular house made from blocks of hard snow. This kind of houses can be found in very cold regions of the world

Block of Flats

It is a one unit in a building comprising of similar housing units. Many people can live in a block of flats at the same time

Caravan

Houses on wheels are called Caravans. They can be pulled by an animal or by a car or truck. People move from one place to another and live in caravans

Tent

Tents are temporary housing that are used for holiday camping. Modern day tents are made of water proof materials.

Stilt House

This is a type of house mostly found in regions with heavy rainfall and frequent flood. These houses are raised on stilts over the surface of water so that the house remains protected from the flood water.

Rooms in a house

Houses have so many different types of rooms that it can be difficult to keep track of all of them. Each room has its function, and people use different rooms in their houses for different reasons.

1. Bedroom

The bedrooms are one of the most important rooms you will have in the house. It is a room where people sleep and keep their personal belongings. Some houses have their bedrooms on the second floor in a duplex. There are large master bedrooms and there are smaller bedrooms that will be perfectly-sized for children.

2. Living Room

This particular room is considered to be the heart of the house. The living room is where the family spends a lot of time. Most people have lots of seats in the living room area to comfortably sit and watch television. A living room is usually the first room that you see when entering a home.

3. Kitchen

A house without a kitchen would be called a complete home. A kitchen is a room that is used all the time. This is where all the delicious dishes would be prepared. Some kitchens are so large that they have chairs and tables setup so that a family can eat food after being prepare. 

4. Bathroom and Toilet

The bathroom and toilet are just as important as any other room. The  bathroom and toilet are rooms where you will be able to perfom body hygiene practices like brushing your teeth and bathing. The toilet is used for urinating and defecating. Some bathrooms have showers and bath tubs

5. Store

The store room is used to keep food items for later use. It is located very close to the kitchen so that anybody that is cooking will have easy access to food materials. Store rooms should be kept neat and tidy to prevent rodents and insects from contaminating the food items.

There are many other examples of rooms that can be found in the house. They are - Dining room, play room, library, nursery and many more.
