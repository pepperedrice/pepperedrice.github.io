Grade 3

Welcome to the class, pick a subject and begin a lesson. 

After completing a lesson, attempt the short quiz that follows immediately. You can go back to any lesson you have completed. You will need a notebook and a pencil to write down any new thing you have learned. If you come across any new word, write it down and search for the meaning.  

Get your note book and prepare for class.
