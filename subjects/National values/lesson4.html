Dress codes

Lesson 4

A good student is required to always keep a good apperance in school and at home.

A good apperance improves a student's behavior and performance and shows that he or she is committed to learning. 

Parents and guardians are also responsible for assisting children in making apperance choices regarding school and personal apperance. 

Torn clothes, sleeve-less shirts, mini skirts and ripped jeans are not allowed to be worn as school uniforms. 
The Hazelwood Academy Dress codes

All students are required and expected to maintain the highest possible standards of personal hygiene. 

Black shoes must be adorned with the school uniform.

Personal sweaters are not allowed.

Every student must be smartly dressed and tucked in at all times. 

Avoid soiling your clothes with cooking oil at break time. 

Avoid playing with dirty equipments on the playground
