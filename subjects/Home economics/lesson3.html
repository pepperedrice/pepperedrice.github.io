Cleaning special rooms in the House

In this lesson, we will discuss different types of special rooms in the house and reasons for cleaning them. 

Special rooms in the house are rooms that are used for specific functions. Special rooms in the house include the bathroom and toilet, kitchen and stores.

Toilet and Bathroom

The toilet and bathroom are special rooms in the house where personal hygiene takes place. People wash themselves, urinate and defecate in the toilet and bathrooms. 

There are different types of toilet which include water closet, pit toilet, bucket toilet. 

Water closet

Pit toilet

Cleaning the toilet 

Types of bathrooms include modern bathrooms with showers and bath tubs, traditional bathrooms.

Modern bathrooms

Cleaning the bathroom

Cleaning the toilet and bathroom is very essential to the health of the family and individuals.
Reasons for cleaning the bathroom and toilet

We clean our bathrooms and toilets to prevent bad odour, to be neat, tidy and convenient for use. 

A clean toilet reduces the risk of getting infected by germs and bacteria. When a guest visits your house, they would be happy to use your clean bathroom and toilet. A clean bathroom and toilet increases the health and living condition of the family

Kitchen and Store

The kitchen is the heart of the home. They can be small or large, but it is where all the meals are made. Kitchens are for cooking and  children are not welcomed to play in the kitchen because cooking utensils like knives are kept in the kitchen and can cause terrible accidents. 

Food stores

Food stores are rooms in the house where food items are kept for later use. 

Types of kitchens

There are mainly two types of kitchens. They include Traditional and Modern kitchens.

Traditional Kitchens 

Traditional kitchens are found in rural areas and villages. They are usually seperate from the building of the house. They make use of firewood, charcoal, kerosene and stoves to cook. 

They are constructed with clay or bricks and iron sheets. Traditional stores are made with the same materials. They make use of items like big pots, baskets and calabash to store food items and kitchen utensils.  

Modern Kitchens

Modern kitchens are found in the urban areas. They are located in the house as a part of the building. They make use of gas cookers, stoves, electric appliances and ovens. Most modern kitchens are fitted with shelves and cabinets for storage. 

They are constructed with tiles, marbles and concrete. Modern houses have pipes for water and sinks. Modern stores are seperate rooms in the house very close to the kitchen. 

Why should we clean the kitchen?

We clean the kitchen and shelves to keep a clean house and to reduce the invasion of rodents and poisonous insects. The kitchen is where all the food is  made so its important to clean the kitchen to prevent contracting illnesses or food poisoning. We also clean the kitchen to avoid accidents

How to clean the kitchen and store

1. Open kitchen doors and windows for proper ventilation

2. Dust all surfaces of the cabinets and shelves with a damp towel

3. Dust electrical appliances in the kitchen. Make sure the electric sockets are switched off.

4. Clean the stove tops and remove ashes from the fire place. 

5. Dust and remove littered food particles from hidden corners of the store

6. Sweep and mop the floor with detergents and disinfectants

7. Empty the recycle bin

8. Arrange all the kitchen utensils in the right place.
