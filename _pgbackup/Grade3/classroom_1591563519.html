Helpful hints

A compound word is made when two words are joined together to make a new word.
For example:
1. news + paper = newspaper
2. eye + brow = eyebrow
3. hand + bag = handbag 

Practice: Use these words to form compound words of your own.
Cup, Air, Foot, Home, Space
