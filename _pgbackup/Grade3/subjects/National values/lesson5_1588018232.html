National Values

Respect for Symbols

Lesson 5

Showing respect for ordinary symbols will attract respect to yourself. 

If you observe your surroundings, you will notice that we dont only have national symbols all round us. Logos that represent companies and organizations are called ordinary symbols. They are easily recognizable and they remind you immediately about the owner of that logo. 

Showing respect for symbols is a sign of respect and it helps you to demonstrate responsibility. You can only show respect if you have respect. Traffic signs are also some examples of ordinary symbols, but they are meany for your safety, so it is a good thing to memorize them. 

Did you know?

"...that a logo can be designed by anybody that has a creative mind"

Respect for National symbols

Showing respect for National symbols is a sign of respect and responsibility and it shows that you are a good citizen of your country. 

Its a duty to respect our national symbols to promote peace and unity. It demonstrates patriotism and nationalism. You can imagine that if Dr, Nnamdi Azikiwe did not respect the national flag, Nigeria would not have gained independence.

When you respect symbols, you will be respected.
