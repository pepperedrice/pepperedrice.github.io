Cleaning the House

Lesson 2

The different types of cleaning materials used in housekeeping

Articles or materials needed to clean the house incluse Brooms, hard brush, napkins, foams, moping sticks, towels, parkers for dust and dirt, waste baskets, cleaning agents, etc. 

Each of the cleaning material and cleaning agents has a specific purpose and should only be used as they are supposed to be used, otherwise accidents could occur. 

Cleaning Agents

Cleaning agents are substances in liquid or powdery form used to remove dirt including dust, stains, bad smells and other contaminants from our home surfaces. Some cleaning agents can kill bacteria and other agents contain substances that help to dissolve oils and grease. They are classified into two: 

General steps in cleaning the house

1. Gather all the materials needed for cleaning

2. Ensure that there is proper ventilation to reduce air pollution from dust

3. Sweep dirt from all corners of the room

4. Remove cobwebs from hidden corners of the house

5. Dust all surfaces and items in the house

6. Mop the floor with a mop, water, detergents and disinfectants

7. Arrange the items in the room accordingly.

Local Cleaning Agents

These are cleaning agents made from local or traditional materials available in our surroundings. Some local cleaning agents include

  • 1.Salt
  • 2. Ashes
  • 3. Ground snail shell
  • 4. Ground coconut shell
  • 5. Ground egg shells 
  • 6. Sharp sand 
    • Modern Cleaning Agents

    These are cleaning agents made from chemical substances and industrial materials. They can be dangerous to our health when they are not used peoperly. Some modern cleaning agents include

  • 1.Bar soap
  • 2. Liquid soap
  • 3. Scrubbing powder 
  • 4. Ground coconut shell
  • 5. Ground egg shells 
  • 6. Sharp sand 
    • Why should we clean the house?
  • A clean house is very essential for people to live healthy lives. We clean the house and our rooms to prevent germs and diseases from infecting us. 
    When we clean our house, we make sure that insects and rodents are not welcome in our rooms. A clean house improves the general health of the family and the community. When your house is clean, visitors are welcome and it shows that you can be respected and responsible.
